Do I need a referral to book? +

No referral is needed. Contact us directly by phone or email and arrange an appointment with the therapist of your choice.

Where are you located? +

24 O'Connell Avenue, Berkeley Road, Dublin 7, D07 XV57. Five minutes' walk from the city centre, LUAS at Grangegorman and Phibsborough, and free parking on Sundays.

Is everything confidential? +

Yes. Every session takes place in a private, confidential and supportive environment with an experienced practitioner.

How do I know which therapy is right for me? +

If you're unsure, just get in touch. We'll talk through what you're going through and point you toward the practitioner and approach best suited to you.
